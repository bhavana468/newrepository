  • Crop Prediction

    Crop Prediction involves predicting the best crop for cultivating from available historical data like weather parameter, soil parameter and historic crop yield. The prediction will help farmer to predict the crop before cultivating onto the agriculture field. The proposed project provides solution to predict crop field from previous data. This research focuses on creation of a prediction model which may be used to future prediction of crop and crop yield. The main objective of crop prediction is to give pace to the growth rate of agriculture development and crop production and productivity which will strengthen the economic status of the farmers and uplift their life-style. To maintain a sustainable growth rate in agriculture production and food safety, extension of new techniques of agriculture and settlement of agriculture related problems of the farmer.
